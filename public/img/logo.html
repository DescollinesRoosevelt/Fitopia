Printable gym logos by Canva

At-home workouts are popular, but many people still prefer the gym because of its convenience. All the equipment they need is there, plus there’s the option of working out with a coach. But competition is fierce; gyms are opening left and right, so if you’re running a gym, you need a powerful gym logo design to stand out.

Free gym logo designs are hard to come by, but with Canva, you have a ton of free options. You have your pick of gym logo templates, all created by our professional designers. Get the perfect premade design for your big box gym, powerlifting gym, fitness center, group fitness studio, sports facility, and more. The gallery of templates contains a number of unisex gym logo designs, but you can easily edit them according to your clientele. In addition, Canva’s online design editor features a premium library of design elements that you can add to your logo template. Search for classic gym vector icons like dumbbells, kettlebells, or a person’s physique. Drag and drop these to your gym logo template and finish the design with the name of your gym. Add your slogan and apply a new font. Change the colors to follow your branding. Want to work on the logo with your team? Send them a link to the gym logo template so that they can edit it, too.

Once you’re done editing the template, download your fresh gym logo as a PDF, PNG, or JPG file. Share the logo with your colleagues via social media or email. Or publish it directly to your social media accounts. You can now create ads, posters, banners, and other marketing materials. Use your new gym logo on your social media content and place them on your merch. If you want high-quality merch like stickers, T-shirts, or hoodies with your logo, order from Canva Print. You’ll get awesome gym merch, emblazoned with your new logo, delivered right to your doorstep. A logo made on Canva is the best way to get your gym’s branding up and running.
